PRIVACY

What we won't do

Our Privacy Policy is very simple - We won't sell, share or disclose any of your Profile information or settings to anyone outside My Community Life (MCL)- PERIOD.    

What we do

Regarding organization's that you're following: The only information anyone will see from the Profile information that you provided or your settings is your name, and this will ONLY be visible to the organizations (also called "groups") you choose to follow (Your fellow Followers will be able to see your name as well). If, as a Follower, you have opted out of receiving notices from an organization, the organization's Administrators will be able to view this designation. Otherwise, we do not disclose any of your Profile information or account settings with organizations registered with MCL.

Regarding businesses: Unless you opt out of receiving offers from one or more businesses, businesses will be able to send you offers based on your consumer Profile answers, but they won't know who you are, and they won't know any information about you except that you are part of a demographic group they chosen to make a particular offer. Lastly, the businesses you choose to follow will NOT know who you are nor will they know any information about you except that you (as a MCL User) have selected them as a business you like and that you would like to get any special offers they send to their Followers. Otherwise, we do not disclose any of your Profile information or account settings with any registered businesses or businesses subscribing to MCL.
